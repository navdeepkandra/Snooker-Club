What is Snooker Actually means?


Snooker is a cue sport played on a rectangular billiards table

covered with a green cloth called baize, with six pockets, one at each corner and

one in the middle of each long side.

Is a Snooker Club a Profitable Business in India?


Well, this is a subjective question! Because the hourly charges of any snooker

table vary from place to place.Usually, an hour at a snooker table in India costs about Rs. 200 to Rs. 300, irrespective of

the number of people playing. So, if you even start with 3 tables, each used for about 5 hours per day can bring you Rs. 3000

to Rs. 4500 daily. That means a revenue around Rs. 90,000 to Rs. 1 lakh each month.
